47 applications for our last senior backend role. 3 interviewed. 1 hired.

Some numbers that tell a different story than the "talent shortage" narrative:

- Time-to-hire: 38 days (industry median: 74)
- Offer acceptance rate: 92%
- First-year retention: 96%

Here's what we do differently:

**We publish the salary band.** €85k-€110k, on the ad. Removes 60% of the tire-kickers before they apply.

**We pay for the take-home test.** €400 for 4 hours. Candidates take it seriously. We take their time seriously. Nobody wins if the process is abusive.

**We do one technical round, not three.** Senior engineers don't need to prove they can binary-search. They need to prove they can reason about tradeoffs. One 90-minute conversation does that.

**We tell everyone a decision within 5 business days.** Including the "no" candidates. Usually with specific feedback. Our Glassdoor reviews from people we didn't hire are 4.1/5.

The talent is there. Recruiting processes are broken.
