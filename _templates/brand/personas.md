# Personas — {{COMPANY_NAME}}

Source of truth for target audiences. Every producing role reads this before drafting content aimed at a specific persona. The `content-strategy` skill uses it to balance cross-channel coverage.

## Design principle

Each persona is **one real, concrete person** you can picture — not a demographic segment. "Thomas, 45, VP IT at a mid-market industrial group in Lyon" beats "the IT decision maker."
